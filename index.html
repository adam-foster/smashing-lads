Smashing Lads

Smashing Lads are an English alternative rock band formed in North Manchester in 2013. Their songs reflect the shared views and experiences of regular, disillusioned blokes. The 9 to 5, the commute, the mortgage and the unattainable dream of escaping the rat race. Their sound is honest and unpretentious, their lyrics observational, witty and at times cynical.

  • Mark Burke Bass, Vocals
  • Joe Whelan Guitar, Vocals
  • Keith Unwin Drums, Vocals
  • Peter Wood Piano, Vocals
